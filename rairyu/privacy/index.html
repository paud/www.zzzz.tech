本隐私政策说明 RaiRyu（以下简称"本 App"）如何处理你的信息。我们尊重并保护每一位用户的隐私。

1. 我们不收集的信息

本 App 不会收集、存储、传输或共享以下信息：

  • 姓名、邮箱、电话号码等个人身份信息
  • 位置信息
  • 联系人、相册、日历等设备数据
  • 用户行为追踪数据，不用于广告投放
  • 不与任何第三方共享用户数据

2. 我们可能访问的本地数据

为了实现 App 的核心功能，本 App 可能在你的设备本地访问以下数据，这些数据不会上传到我们的服务器：

  • UserDefaults：保存你的 App 偏好设置（如界面状态、缓存配置）
  • 本地文件缓存：缓存 Web 内容以加快加载速度并支持离线访问
  • WKWebView Cookie / Storage：浏览功能所需的标准 Web 存储，仅在设备本地

3. 网络通信

当 App 加载 Web 内容时，会向相应的服务器发起标准 HTTP/HTTPS 请求。这些请求由你访问的网页域名所有者按其自身的隐私政策处理，与本 App 开发者无关。

4. 第三方服务

本 App 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

5. 儿童隐私

本 App 不针对 13 岁以下儿童设计，也不会有意收集任何儿童信息。

6. 数据安全

所有本地缓存的数据均存储在 iOS 系统提供的沙盒环境中，受 iOS 系统级权限保护。App 卸载后，所有数据将被系统自动清除。

7. 政策变更

如本政策发生重大变更,我们将在本页面公告新版本及生效日期。继续使用本 App 即表示你接受更新后的政策。

8. 联系方式

如果你对本隐私政策有任何疑问,请联系：

  • 邮箱：privacy@zzzz.tech

This Privacy Policy explains how RaiRyu (the "App") handles your information. We respect and protect the privacy of every user.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, or other device data
  • Behavioral tracking data — we do not serve ads
  • We do not share user data with any third party

2. Local Data We May Access

To deliver core functionality, the App may access the following data locally on your device only — never uploaded to our servers:

  • UserDefaults: stores app preferences (UI state, cache config)
  • Local file cache: caches web content for faster loading and offline access
  • WKWebView cookies / storage: standard web storage required for browsing, kept only on-device

3. Network Communication

When loading web content, the App issues standard HTTP/HTTPS requests to the relevant servers. Those requests are governed by the privacy policies of the websites you visit, which are independent of this App's developer.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from them.

6. Data Security

All locally cached data is stored within the iOS sandbox and protected by system-level permissions. Uninstalling the App removes all of its data automatically.

7. Policy Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will post the updated version and its effective date on this page. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
